About Us

IT GURUKUL - Institute of Computer Science and Technology is a Training Partner of EduWeb Solutions started by a Computer Engineer Er VR Chauhan in 2014 to provide high quality computer training with latest technology and in easiest way for rural area students, unemployed youths, housewives and all others who wants to learn computer. Our Aim is to making Bihar as an "IT Skilled Bihar". Students.
