About Us

IT GURUKUL - Institute of Computer Science and Technology is a Training Partner of EduWeb Solutions started by a Computer Engineer Er VR Chauhan in 2014 to provide high quality computer training with latest technology and in easiest way for rural area students, unemployed youths, housewives and all others who wants to learn computer. Our Aim is to making Bihar as an "IT Skilled Bihar". We Offer Certificate and Diploma Course in Computer Application such as CCA, CFA, CIT, CCP, DCA, DIT, ADCA, HDCA, etc.. and also providing Computer Programming and Web Development Training for Tech and Engg. Students.

Eduweb Solutions are Bihar based Web Design and Development Service Provider specially for Educational Institutions. We are registered under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) having registration no. UDYAM-BR-06-0036326.We Design Responsive Website using BootStrap with Menu, Slider, Notice Panel, Image and Video Gallery, Enquiry Form, Google Map, Google Form and Social Media Integration. We Develop Web Application with Database like Online Registration or Application, Online Examination, Online Payment Integration with existing website, We also Design WordPress Website and Blog for Educational Institutions
